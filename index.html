Acadamic Experience

Summer camp at Xi'an Jiaotong University(XJTU)

July 18, 2023 - July 20, 2023

During my freshman year, our class was given the opportunity to participate in a summer academic camp at Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU), one of China's leading institutions. Though the program lasted only three days, it profoundly immersed us in the rigorous academic environment of this top-tier university. Professors from the School of Mathematics and Statistics delivered presentations on their cutting-edge research fields, providing us with a preliminary understanding of contemporary frontiers in mathematics.This experience solidified my determination to pursue a career in mathematical research.

BIMSA Summer School on Chow-Witt Rings

August 21, 2023 - August 25, 2023

During the summer following my freshman year, I actively explored cutting-edge mathematical fields to identify my academic focus. Attending the Chou Witt Rings Summer School at the Beijing Institute of Mathematical Sciences and Applications introduced me to algebraic geometry—a discipline both ancient and dynamically evolving. While the lectures proved challenging for a first-year student, mentorship from professors and peers clarified my learning trajectory and solidified my resolve to specialize in this field. This experience marked a pivotal transition from the initial uncertainties of university life, opening a gateway to advanced study.

China Systems Science Conference(CSSC)

May 19, 2024 - May 21, 2024

During my sophomore year, our faculty organized a remarkable academic excursion for our class to participate in the China Systems Science Conference. This prestigious event profoundly demonstrated mathematics' interdisciplinary applications and revealed its inherent vitality as a driving force in scientific innovation. Over the course of the conference, we engaged with cutting-edge research spanning computational harmonic analysis, machine learning optimization, and mathematical modeling in engineering sciences. Attending presentations delivered by renowned scholars, academicians, and professors across diverse fields—including physics, biomedicine, and data science—enriched our understanding of how mathematical frameworks underpin modern scientific breakthroughs. The experience not only broadened our academic horizons but also highlighted mathematics' pivotal role in addressing complex challenges within strategic priorities and socioeconomic development.

NTU AI&GPT program

January 12, 2025 - January 19, 2025

During the seven-day AI & GPT academic program at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore, our team actively engaged in lectures on machine learning and deep learning delivered by faculty professors.
